Refund Policy

Last updated: October 11, 2025

Overview

At Knight Logics, we stand behind the quality of our work and are committed to ensuring client satisfaction. This refund policy outlines the terms and conditions under which refunds may be provided for our software development and business automation services.

Service Categories

Custom Software Development Projects

For custom development projects with defined scope and deliverables:

  • Initial Consultation: 100% refundable if cancelled within 24 hours of payment
  • Planning Phase: 50% refundable before development begins
  • Development Phase: Refunds calculated based on work completed and milestones achieved
  • Final Delivery: No refunds after client acceptance of final deliverables

Business Automation Solutions

For automation systems and integrations:

  • Discovery Phase: Full refund available within 72 hours of project start
  • Implementation: Partial refunds based on completion percentage
  • Testing & Deployment: 48-hour satisfaction guarantee after go-live

Consulting Services

For hourly consulting and technical advisory:

  • Hourly Sessions: No refunds for completed sessions
  • Prepaid Packages: Unused hours refundable within 30 days
  • Strategy Sessions: 24-hour cancellation policy with full refund

Satisfaction Guarantee

We offer a satisfaction guarantee for all our services:

  • If you're not satisfied with our work, we'll work with you to make it right
  • Additional revisions at no charge within scope
  • Alternative solutions if initial approach doesn't meet needs
  • Transparent communication throughout the process

Refund Process

To request a refund:

  1. Contact us within the applicable refund period
  2. Provide detailed explanation of dissatisfaction
  3. Allow opportunity for resolution before refund processing
  4. Refunds processed within 5-10 business days via original payment method

Non-Refundable Items

The following are non-refundable:

  • Third-party software licenses or services purchased on your behalf
  • Domain names and hosting services
  • Work completed to your specifications and accepted in writing
  • Services delivered more than 30 days ago
  • Emergency or rush work with agreed-upon premium pricing

Force Majeure

In cases of circumstances beyond our control (natural disasters, pandemics, etc.), we will work with clients to reschedule work or provide prorated refunds as appropriate.

Dispute Resolution

Before pursuing formal dispute resolution:

  • We encourage direct communication to resolve issues
  • Mediation may be considered for complex disputes
  • All refund decisions are at the discretion of Knight Logics management

Payment Method Considerations

Refund processing times may vary by payment method:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 3-5 business days
  • Bank Transfers: 5-7 business days
  • Digital Payments: 1-3 business days
