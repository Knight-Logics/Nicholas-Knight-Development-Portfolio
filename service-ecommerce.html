E-Commerce FAQs

Common questions from businesses before starting a store build or migration.

How much does an e-commerce website cost?

A custom storefront with Snipcart or Stripe, product catalog, and mobile checkout typically starts in the mid four figures. Larger catalogs, inventory automation, and dropshipping integrations add scope. Every build gets a detailed quote after a discovery call.

Do you build on Shopify or custom code?

Both, depending on what fits. Shopify suits larger catalogs needing their ecosystem. For lean, fast storefronts without monthly platform fees, a custom-coded static site with Snipcart or Stripe Checkout often performs better and costs less to run.

Can you migrate an existing store?

Yes. Product data, customer records, URLs, and SEO equity can all be migrated with proper redirects and data mapping. The migration plan is locked in before a single line is written so nothing gets lost.

What payment processors do you integrate with?

Stripe and Snipcart for custom-code builds. Shopify Payments, PayPal, and Stripe for Shopify projects. Subscriptions, split payments, or in-person POS sync are scoped individually based on requirements.
