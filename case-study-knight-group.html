The Build

A local service site tied to a broader growth and operations system

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JS GitHub Pages JSON-LD Schema Google Search Console Photo Gallery

Knight Group operates in a competitive local service market where trust, photos, and Google visibility matter. The site needed to convert visitors into calls — not just look good. Every design and SEO decision was made with that goal in mind.

This sits above a standard Local Launch build because the website is only one layer. Knight Group also runs with a dedicated booking page, separate business phone and email routing, CRM and job-record workflows, automated email follow-up, and supporting social posting systems around the public site.

The build is a multi-page static site with a homepage, about page, galleries, services, booking, pricing, and contact. Photo galleries provide social proof of real work completed. The contact and booking surfaces are optimized for quick phone calls and form submissions.

The technical SEO stack is comprehensive: LocalBusiness schema with full address and service area data, BreadcrumbList on every page, canonical tags, sitemap, and Google Search Console verification — all working together for maximum local search visibility.

What makes this a Growth System (not just Local Launch Plus): Behind the website, Knight Group runs a complete operational ecosystem. Lead intake flows into a CRM that tracks job history, estimate status, and customer communication. Automated email sequences nurture leads while reducing manual follow-up. Social media posting and engagement happens 3x daily across Facebook, X, and LinkedIn — with an autonomous manager handling replies, community engagement, and channel growth. A directory registration foundation gets the business listed on 9+ platforms (BizListUSA, MerchantCircle, Brownbook, EZlocal, etc.) for maximum local search presence. This operational stack is what justifies the $3,500+ investment — the website works harder because it's paired with systems that feed it, follow up on its leads, and amplify its reach.

Operational Stack Behind the Site

The Systems That Make the Website Convert

The website is the front door, but the operational systems are what answer it. Knight Group's Growth System pairs the public site with dedicated backend automation that handles lead capture, follow-up, and outreach — turning one-time visitors into booked jobs and repeat customers.

Lead Management & CRM

All leads captured through the website or direct inquiries flow into a dedicated CRM system. This isn't email-only tracking — it's a full job record workflow:

  • Lead intake from website forms, phone calls, and directory platforms
  • Automatic lead scoring and categorization by service type
  • Job record creation with estimate history, photos, and notes
  • Lead follow-up tracking and status workflows
  • Integration with email automation for triggered sequences
  • Reporting dashboard for pipeline visibility

Automated Email Follow-Up

Once a lead is captured, email sequences automatically nurture them toward a booked estimate or job. These aren't generic broadcasts — they're triggered based on lead behavior and CRM status:

  • Immediate confirmation email after form submission
  • Service-specific follow-up sequences (e.g., plumbing leads get plumbing-focused messaging)
  • ~20 automated email sends per day across all active leads
  • Estimate reminders if no response after 3 days
  • Post-job follow-up for reviews and referrals
  • Professional template design with branding and CTAs

Social Media Automation & Growth

Knight Group's social presence isn't manually managed — it runs on autonomous systems designed to post, engage, and grow followers across multiple platforms:

  • Automated posting to Facebook, X (Twitter), and LinkedIn — 3x daily
  • Post content rotation: service highlights, customer testimonials, local tips, project photos
  • Autonomous social manager: replies to comments within hours (not days)
  • Community engagement: joins local Facebook groups, replies to service-request threads
  • Follower growth optimization: strategic follows, engagement campaigns
  • Lead capture via DMs: direct message replies and inquiry tracking

Directory Registration & Local Presence

Beyond Google and the website, Knight Group is registered on 9+ local business directories with consistent NAP (Name, Address, Phone) data:

  • BizListUSA, MerchantCircle, Brownbook, EZlocal, Alignable
  • Tampa Bay and Safety Harbor city directories
  • Niche local platforms (Patch, BusinessTampaBay)
  • Consistent NAP across all platforms for local search authority
  • Reputation and review monitoring across directories
  • Planned: Nextdoor presence (pending community approval)

Sample Email Sequence Quality

The automated email sequences are professionally designed with clear CTAs, service-specific messaging, and brand-consistent templates. Each sequence is triggered based on lead behavior (form submission, service type, days since contact) and adapts messaging accordingly. This ensures follow-up happens without manual overhead while maintaining professionalism and conversion intent.
