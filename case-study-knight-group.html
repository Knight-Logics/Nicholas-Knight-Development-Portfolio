The Build

Custom Site for a Service Business That Needed to Rank Locally

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JS GitHub Pages JSON-LD Schema Google Search Console Photo Gallery

Knight Group operates in a competitive local service market where trust, photos, and Google visibility matter. The site needed to convert visitors into calls — not just look good. Every design and SEO decision was made with that goal in mind.

The build is a multi-page static site with a homepage, about page, galleries, services, and contact. Photo galleries provide social proof of real work completed. The contact section is optimized for quick phone calls and form submissions.

The technical SEO stack is comprehensive: LocalBusiness schema with full address and service area data, BreadcrumbList on every page, canonical tags, sitemap, and Google Search Console verification — all working together for maximum local search visibility.

What Was Delivered

  • Multi-page site: home, about, services, galleries, contact
  • Photo gallery showcasing completed work — social proof at scale
  • LocalBusiness JSON-LD schema with address, phone, and service area
  • BreadcrumbList schema on all pages — included in Rich Results
  • Organization schema for entity establishment with Google
  • Open Graph + Twitter Card meta on every page
  • Sitemap.xml submitted to Google Search Console
  • 100/100 Accessibility — full compliance from launch day
  • Fully responsive — mobile, tablet, and desktop
