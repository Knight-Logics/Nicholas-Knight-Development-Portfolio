Consultation FAQs

Everything you need to know before you request a time.

What if I am not sure which consultation type fits?

Choose the custom-fit option or the free Website and GBP Audit. Knight Logics can still route the request to the right next step after reviewing the business context.

Is the Website and GBP Audit really free?

Yes. It is meant to help local businesses understand where their current visibility and website issues are actually coming from before committing to a larger scope.

Do I need a finished scope before I request a call?

No. Rough direction is enough. If you know what is broken, what you want to improve, or what kind of business outcome you need, that is enough to have a useful consultation.

Can businesses outside Tampa Bay use this page?

Yes. Knight Logics targets Tampa Bay locally, but remote consultations are available for businesses anywhere. The local focus improves discoverability, not who is allowed to book.
