What Gets Cleaned Up

  • Primary and secondary category review

Best-Fit Situations

When GBP work deserves its own lane

Some businesses have a decent website but a weak or underused profile. Others have a verified profile that still does not convert well because the categories, services, posts, and landing pages are disconnected.

FAQ

Google Business Profile FAQs

Can GBP work help if the site is weak too?

Yes, but the best outcome usually comes from fixing both systems together. If the landing pages are weak, the site still needs attention alongside the profile.

Do you help with review systems?

Yes. Review acquisition and response patterns are part of trust and conversion, so the workflow matters just as much as the review count itself.

Can you help with categories and service areas?

Yes. Those settings are core parts of profile positioning and should match the real business footprint and the site pages supporting it.

What if I need both profile and site fixes?

Then the profile work should be paired with local SEO implementation on the site so both systems reinforce the same local signals.
