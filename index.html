Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to what Tampa Bay business owners ask most before getting started.

How much does a small business website cost?

Scope drives price, but most brochure and lead-generation sites land in the mid four figures. No recurring page-builder fees — every site is hand-coded and owned outright. Contact us for a free scoped quote.

Do you offer local SEO services in Tampa Bay?

Yes. Knight Logics handles Google Business Profile optimization, schema markup, Search Console setup, sitemap submission, and on-page technical SEO for businesses across Tampa, Clearwater, St. Pete, and Safety Harbor.

How do hand-coded sites compare to WordPress or Wix?

Hand-coded sites load faster, score higher on Core Web Vitals, and carry zero plugin vulnerabilities or recurring fees. Knight Logics builds from scratch in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript — lean, fast, and built to rank.

Can you help my business show up on Google Maps?

Yes. Maps visibility comes from a properly optimized Google Business Profile combined with a well-structured site. Knight Logics handles both — GBP setup, category selection, service area configuration, and the technical SEO that validates your local relevance.

Do you work with businesses outside of Tampa Bay?

Yes. Website design, automation, and software development services are available remotely worldwide. On-site collaboration is available for Tampa Bay and Pinellas County clients.
