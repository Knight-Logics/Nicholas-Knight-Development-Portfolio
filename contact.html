Contact FAQs

Quick answers before you send the brief or request a consultation.

Should I use this page or the booking page?

Use this page when you want to send business details first or ask a broader question. Use the booking page when you already want a call and need to request a specific consultation type and preferred time.

How fast will Knight Logics reply?

Most contact requests get a direct response within 24 hours. If timing is urgent, calling or texting is the fastest option.

What should I include in my message?

Include what your business does, what is not working now, what you want to improve, and any timeline or budget you already know. If you have an existing website or Google Business Profile, include that too.

Do you only work with Tampa Bay businesses?

Tampa Bay is the strongest local focus for web design, local SEO, and Google Business Profile work, but remote website, e-commerce, and automation projects are still possible outside the area.

Can I ask about GBP or automation without a full rebuild?

Yes. Local SEO cleanup, Google Business Profile work, audits, automation planning, and internal-tool projects can all be scoped as standalone engagements.
