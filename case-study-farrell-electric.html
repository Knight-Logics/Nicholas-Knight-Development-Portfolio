The Build

Entry-tier multi-page electrician site on GitHub Pages

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JS GitHub Pages Schema Markup

The Preview Launch Site is purpose-built for businesses that need an immediate online presence without the scope (or budget) of a large analytics-rich build. It answers the question: "How can we get online this week?"

Farrell Electric needed a way for potential customers to find contact details, see service offerings, and submit inquiries without a custom domain or complex backend. The delivered build uses a lean 3-page structure deployed on free GitHub Pages hosting.

This tier skips advanced analytics, deep local directory rollout, and heavy SEO engineering while still focusing on conversion: clear value prop, services listed, work photos, and a contact form. It's built for immediate lead capture and clean handoff to future upgrades.

At $500, the Preview Launch Site represents the absolute minimum entry point into custom-coded, professional online presence. It's ideal for sole proprietors, new service businesses, and contractors testing the market before investing in a fuller local SEO buildout.

What Was Delivered

  • Three custom HTML pages with responsive design
  • HTML5 semantic markup for accessibility
  • Basic SEO meta tags (title, description, OG, Twitter Card)
  • Electrician schema (JSON-LD) for service type identification
  • Contact form with email routing
  • Services section with clear offerings
  • Work/portfolio gallery showing completed projects
  • Service area and geographic coverage
  • About section for trust and credibility
  • Hamburger navigation for mobile
  • Mobile-responsive layout (no desktop-only design)
  • Deployed on free GitHub Pages hosting

What's Not Included

  • Custom domain (hosted on GitHub Pages URL)
  • Google Analytics or Microsoft Clarity
  • Google Business Profile setup
  • Deep content architecture (service clusters, city-page network, blog)
  • Advanced SEO foundation (keywords, local indexing focus)
  • Directory registration or citation work
  • Bing Webmaster setup
  • Local-specific service pages

Ideal For

Preview Launch Site is Right When:

  • You need to go online this week, not this quarter
  • Your business is new and you want to test market demand before heavy investment
  • You're a sole proprietor or small team with limited upfront budget
  • You want a professional online presence but don't need SEO optimization yet
  • Most of your lead flow comes from word-of-mouth or direct referrals
  • You'd rather start simple and upgrade to a fuller SEO build later
