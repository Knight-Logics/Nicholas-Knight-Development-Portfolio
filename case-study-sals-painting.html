The Build

Full technical SEO foundation without automation overhead

HTML5 CSS3 Vanilla JS GA4 Analytics Clarity Heatmaps Google Search Console

The Search-Ready Starter bridges the gap between entry-level (Preview Launch Site) and full-service local builds (Local Launch Plus). It's built for contractors, painters, and service providers who want Google organic visibility — but don't yet need CRM integration, automated follow-up, or social media automation.

Sal's Painting got a search-optimized website with service, gallery, FAQ, and contact content plus the core technical SEO plumbing: GA4 for conversion tracking, Clarity for user behavior, and Google Search Console verification, plus a brand identity package (cards, brochures, logo) to back up the web presence.

The key differentiator from Preview Launch: this is about being found when someone searches "painter near me" or "interior painting [service area]." No shortcuts on analytics and no missed Search Console setup.

Pre-existing Google Business Profile support was also improved with aligned schema and category optimization, giving the site and GBP a cohesive local identity without requiring from-scratch GBP creation.

What Was Delivered

  • Custom domain registration and setup
  • Search-optimized website architecture with conversion-focused service, gallery, about, contact, and FAQ content
  • Service-focused sections (interior, exterior, drywall, accent walls, deck staining, renovations)
  • LocalBusiness JSON-LD schema with service area and address
  • FAQPage schema for rich snippets
  • OfferCatalog and Offer schema layered into the homepage content
  • Google Analytics 4 with custom configuration
  • Microsoft Clarity heatmaps and session recording
  • Google Search Console verification and sitemapsubmission
  • Robots.txt and sitemap.xml optimization
  • Service-area keyword targeting (towns, neighborhoods)
  • Contact form with email routing
  • Mobile-responsive design (mobile-first)
  • Open Graph + Twitter Card meta on all pages
  • Professional branding package (business cards, brochures, logo refinement)
  • Pre-existing GBP integration and optimization

What's Not Included

  • CRM or lead management system integration
  • Automated email follow-up sequences
  • Social media automation or posting
  • Directory and citation registration (BizListUSA, MerchantCircle, etc.)
  • Ongoing SEO maintenance or content updates
  • Social media manager integration
  • Paid ad setup (Google Ads, Facebook Ads)
  • Bing Webmaster Tools setup and verification
