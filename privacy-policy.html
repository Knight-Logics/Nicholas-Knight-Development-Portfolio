Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 7, 2026

Who We Are

Knight Logics is a web design and local SEO service based in Tampa Bay, Florida. We build custom websites, e-commerce stores, and automation systems for small businesses. Our website is https://knightlogics.com and you can reach us at support@knightlogics.com.

Information We Collect

We collect information in the following ways:

Contact and Intake Forms

When you fill out a contact form, consultation request, or package intake form on our site, we collect:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Business name and website URL (if provided)
  • Project details, goals, and any notes you submit

These form submissions are delivered to us via Formspree (formspree.io). Formspree receives your submission data as part of that delivery. See Formspree's Privacy Policy.

Payment Information

When you purchase a package through our website, payments are processed by Stripe (stripe.com). We do not store or handle your card number, CVC, or full billing details. Stripe collects and secures that data directly. See Stripe's Privacy Policy. We receive a confirmation of payment and the package purchased.

Analytics and Usage Data

We use two analytics tools to understand how visitors use our website:

  • Google Analytics (GA4) — Tracks page views, session data, traffic sources, and general browsing behavior. Data is anonymized and aggregated. See Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Microsoft Clarity — Records sessions, heatmaps, and click behavior to help us improve site usability. Clarity may record screen interactions on our site. See Microsoft's Privacy Statement.

Technical Data

Standard web server and analytics data including IP address, browser type, device type, operating system, and referring URL is collected automatically when you visit our site.

How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to your inquiry and discuss your project
  • To process your payment and begin your website project
  • To send project-related communications (not marketing unless you opt in)
  • To analyze and improve how our website performs and converts
  • To comply with legal obligations

Information Sharing

We do not sell or rent your personal information. We share data only with the third-party services listed above (Formspree, Stripe, Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity) as necessary to operate the site and process your order. Each of those services has their own privacy policy governing how they handle data.

We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our legal rights.

Data Retention

We retain contact form submissions and project intake data for the duration of your project plus a reasonable period afterward for record-keeping. Analytics data retention is governed by each provider's settings. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of your personal data we hold. To exercise these rights, email us at support@knightlogics.com.

Cookies

Our website uses cookies set by Google Analytics, Microsoft Clarity, and Stripe (during checkout). For full details on what cookies are set and how to control them, see our Cookie Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Continued use of the site after changes constitutes acceptance.
