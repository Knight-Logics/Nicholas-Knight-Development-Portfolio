Website FAQs

Short answers to the questions most businesses ask before starting a rebuild or a new launch.

How much does web design cost in Tampa Bay?

Knight Logics uses a clear upgrade ladder so you only pay for what you actually need: Starter Preview Site ($500), Starter Live Site ($700), Search-Ready Starter ($1,200), Local Launch Website ($1,997), Local Launch Plus ($2,997+), and Growth System (custom from $3,500). Monthly maintenance plans start at $197/mo.

How much does a custom website usually cost?

A quick custom-coded preview site starts at $500 — no domain, no Google setup, just a clean professional page. Adding domain and live hosting brings it to $700. Adding the full Google technical setup (Search Console, schema, analytics) brings it to $1,200. The full Local Launch with multi-page design, GBP alignment, and everything launch-ready is $1,997. Larger builds and growth systems start at $2,997.

What's the difference between a custom-coded site and a template or website builder?

Template sites (Wix, Squarespace, WordPress themes) load unnecessary code, share hosting signals with thousands of other sites, and limit what you can do. A custom-coded site is built only for your business — cleaner, faster, easier to rank, and fully yours to control.

Can an existing WordPress or builder site be redesigned?

Yes. Content, forms, analytics, and important URLs can be migrated into a faster custom-coded site while preserving what already works and cleaning up what does not.

Do you handle Google Business Profile and Search Console setup?

Yes. Knight Logics can handle Google Business alignment, GA4, Search Console, sitemap submission, schema markup, and the technical SEO basics needed for a clean launch.

How long does it take to build a custom website?

A typical brochure site for a Tampa Bay small business takes 2–4 weeks from discovery call to live deployment. Larger multi-page builds and sites with custom functionality can take 4–8 weeks. Timeline depends heavily on how quickly content and feedback are provided.

What happens after launch?

You can take a full handoff and own the site outright, or keep Knight Logics involved for ongoing updates, SEO cleanup, performance work, and follow-on automation.

Do you work with businesses outside Tampa Bay?

Yes — every project is built remotely and deployed to GitHub Pages, Vercel, or your preferred host. While the primary focus is Tampa Bay, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, and Pinellas County, web design and local SEO projects are taken on statewide and nationally for the right fit.
