The Growth System tier pairs a deep service-page website with CRM workflows, automated email sequences, and social media automation.

Operational Stack Behind the Site

Complete Growth System — CRM, Email, Social, and Directories

Screen Team's Growth System is identical in operational scope to Knight Group: the website attracts leads, but the backend systems ensure they're captured, nurtured, and followed up on — while social media and directories amplify reach. This is what justifies the $3,500+ investment.

Lead Management & CRM

All Screen Team quote requests flow into a dedicated CRM system that tracks every inquiry, estimate, and job:

  • Lead intake from website forms, phone calls, and directory platforms
  • Automatic lead scoring and categorization by service (rescreens, pool enclosures, etc.)
  • Job record creation with estimate history, photos, and customer notes
  • Lead follow-up tracking and status workflows
  • Integration with email automation for triggered sequences
  • Reporting dashboard for pipeline visibility

Automated Email Follow-Up

Once a quote request comes in, email sequences automatically nurture Screen Team leads toward a booked job:

  • Immediate confirmation email after quote form submission
  • Service-specific follow-up (pool enclosure leads get pool-specific messaging, etc.)
  • ~20 automated email sends per day across all active leads
  • Quote reminder emails if no response after 3-4 days
  • Post-job follow-up for reviews and referrals
  • Professional templates with brand and direct contact info

Social Media Automation & Growth

Screen Team's social presence runs on autonomous systems designed to post daily, engage with followers, and grow community reach:

  • Automated posting to Facebook, X (Twitter), and LinkedIn — 3x daily
  • Content rotation: project showcases, service tips, customer testimonials, before/afters
  • Autonomous social manager: replies to comments within hours
  • Community engagement: joins local Facebook groups, replies to service requests
  • Nextdoor presence: recommendations and community visibility in service areas
  • Follower growth optimization: strategic engagement and relationship building

Directory Registration & Local Presence

Beyond the website and Google, Screen Team is registered on 9+ local business directories with consistent NAP data:

  • BizListUSA, MerchantCircle, Brownbook, EZlocal, Alignable
  • Tampa Bay, St. Petersburg, Clearwater, Safety Harbor directories
  • Pinellas County niche platforms (Patch, BusinessTampaBay)
  • Nextdoor community recommendations and visibility
  • Consistent NAP across all platforms for local search authority
  • Review monitoring and response across all directories
