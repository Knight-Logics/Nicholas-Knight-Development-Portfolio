ScreenTeamLLC.com Case Study

A local service-business website built to make one thing easy: turn local search traffic into direct calls and quote requests with clear trust signals and service-page depth.

Snapshot

Project Type

Local service website for a Tampa Bay screen repair and enclosure business.

Primary Objectives
  • Clarify offer and service scope fast
  • Push direct call/text quote actions
  • Build local trust and credibility
  • Support local SEO discoverability
What Was Built

From basic web presence to lead-gen structure

The site was structured around service clarity and conversion intent, not generic design fluff. Core services are split into dedicated pages, with strong call or text CTAs and a quote-focused contact flow. The content architecture supports both user clarity and local indexability.

  • Dedicated service pages for high-intent service categories
  • Direct contact architecture with call/text-first CTAs
  • FAQ and local-business schema for stronger search understanding
  • Trust pages (about, gallery, guarantee, contact) to reduce hesitation
Business Outcome

The resulting site presents the business as established, direct, and trustworthy - a stronger local conversion surface than a basic social profile or generic one-page site.
