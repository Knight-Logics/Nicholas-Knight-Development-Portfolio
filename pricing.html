Pricing FAQs

Quick answers for Tampa Bay website pricing, local SEO scope, Google Business work, and deposit options.

How much does a small business website cost in Tampa Bay?

Starter sites begin at $500, live brochure-style sites start at $700, search-ready builds start at $1,200, and full local launch packages start at $1,997. The final number depends on launch setup depth, page count, and local SEO scope.

Which package is best if I need better Google rankings?

The Search Foundation and Local Launch packages are the strongest fit when you need service-page SEO, schema, Search Console setup, and Google Business alignment. Businesses needing city pages or stronger map-pack visibility usually need Local Launch or the related add-ons.

Can I pay a deposit instead of the full amount?

Yes. Stripe checkout is used for all package payments. Several larger packages also offer deposit-based checkout so you can reserve kickoff first and pay the remaining balance during project milestones.

Do you handle Google Business Profile optimization and schema markup?

Yes. Google Business Profile cleanup, local SEO setup, schema markup, citation work, and Search Console setup can be bundled into a build or purchased as standalone work.

Can I hire Knight Logics for SEO or GBP work on an existing site?

Yes. You do not need a full rebuild first. Existing sites can get standalone schema cleanup, service-area SEO pages, Google Business Profile work, citations, audits, and local visibility support.

What if I need e-commerce, CRM, or automation instead of a brochure site?

Use the e-commerce and growth-system sections as your starting point. Knight Logics also builds storefronts, job record systems, dashboards, CRM-style tools, and workflow automations when a business needs more than a standard website.
