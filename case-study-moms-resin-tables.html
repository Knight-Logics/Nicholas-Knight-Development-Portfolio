The Build

Full e-commerce platform with product catalog, custom builder, and user authentication

HTML5 CSS3 JavaScript Stripe API User Auth Google Merchant GA4 Analytics Merchant Feed

Mom's Resin Tables is fundamentally different from service-based builds. This is a product-commerce system where customers need to browse a catalog, compare options, build custom pieces, checkout with payment processing, and track their orders.

The E-Commerce Launch tier includes all the technical complexity of a working storefront: product database, shopping cart with localStorage persistence, Stripe payment integration, user authentication system for order history, a custom tabletop builder with configurable options (wood type, resin color, base style, finish), and dedicated flows for both ready-made catalog purchases and commission/custom orders.

The build also includes policy pages (shipping, lead times, custom build process, refund policy, terms), Google Merchant Center product feed integration, analytics and conversion tracking (GA4, Clarity, Pinterest pixel), and commerce metadata support aimed at search visibility. Current live DOM verification on momsresintables.com shows active JSON-LD with 16 schema type families on the homepage.

E-Commerce Feature Breakdown

What Makes This a Full-Featured Storefront

User Account & Order Tracking

Unlike simple payment-link sites, Mom's Resin offers persistent user accounts where customers can track their orders, view order history, and manage their account:

  • User registration and secure authentication
  • Login/logout with persistent session
  • Order history dashboard for tracking past purchases
  • Custom order/commission status tracking
  • Account profile management (address, contact info)
  • Password reset and account security

Custom Tabletop Builder

A unique feature that differentiates the site: customers can design their own custom tabletop by selecting from configurable options:

  • Wood type selection (available species and finishes)
  • Resin color/style options
  • Base design and material options
  • Finish and edge treatment choices
  • Real-time price calculator
  • Commission intake form after design creation

Payment & Checkout System

Full Stripe integration handles both catalog and custom order payments:

  • Stripe checkout for ready-made catalog items
  • Cart review before payment
  • Multiple payment methods (cards, digital wallets)
  • Order confirmation and receipt emails
  • Commission payment link generation (for custom orders)
  • Refund policy enforcement and transaction logging

Google Merchant Center & Discovery

Products are syndicated to Google for maximum discovery and sales reach:

  • Dynamic XML product feed (Google Merchant Center)
  • Product-detail feed attributes (name, price, image, availability) with active Product/Offer schema exposure on live pages
  • Offer-ready pricing and availability metadata in the feed layer
  • Google Shopping integration for search and feed ads
  • Product reviews and ratings capability
  • Rich-result eligibility depends on restoring the current public JSON-LD layer

Trust & Transparency

Because handcrafted products require buyer confidence, the site emphasizes transparency across every decision point: detailed shipping policies, clear lead time expectations, honest custom build process documentation, and straightforward refund policies. These trust elements reduce buyer hesitation and support higher perceived value.
