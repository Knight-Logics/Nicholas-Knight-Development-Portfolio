Mom's Resin Tables Case Study

A handcrafted product-brand site built to look premium, support custom order intake, and make a niche e-commerce business feel established and trustworthy.

Snapshot

Project Type

E-commerce-style website and custom quote system for a handcrafted product brand.

Primary Objectives
  • Position the brand as premium and credible
  • Showcase one-of-one products cleanly
  • Capture custom build and quote demand
  • Support nationwide-shipping sales conversations
What Was Built

E-commerce presentation with quote-first conversion flow

Instead of a generic template storefront, the build focuses on premium product presentation, clear product and policy framing, and a practical custom-build intake process. It balances visual storytelling with direct response actions for buyers who need custom work, not just off-the-shelf checkout.

  • Branded catalog and product showcase layout
  • Custom-build and commission quote intake forms
  • Policy, FAQ, and support pages that reduce buyer friction
  • Structured messaging for handcrafted, one-of-one product sales
Business Outcome

The final result gives the business a stronger online sales foundation: better trust, clearer offer structure, and easier quote conversion than social-only or marketplace-only presence.
